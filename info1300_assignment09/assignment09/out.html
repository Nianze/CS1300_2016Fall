Glossary of Basic Archery Terms

End
A group of arrows shot during a tournament. Usually 6.
Nock (noun)
A notch at the rear of an arrow. The bow string is placed in the nock.
Nock (verb)
To place an arrow against the string prior to shooting.
Serving
Thread wound around a bow string to protect the string.
